Our Story

Born from a love of backyard boils and coastal living, The Salty Claw brings authentic, no-frills Cajun seafood to the neighborhood. We source the freshest ingredients, mix up our secret spice blend, and serve it all up with a side of Southern hospitality. Come hang out, get messy, and taste the tradition!
